## What you can access

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Articles" icon="newspaper" color="#01B2FF" href="/api-reference/introduction">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Stories" icon="layers" color="#01B2FF" href="/api-reference/introduction">
    AI-generated clusters of related articles about the same event or topic.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Companies" icon="building" color="#01B2FF" href="/api-reference/introduction">
    Nordic company profiles with stock listings, FIGI identifiers, and exchange data.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Countries" icon="globe" color="#01B2FF" href="/api-reference/introduction">
    Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Finland, and Iceland with associated exchanges and articles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Exchanges" icon="chart-line" color="#01B2FF" href="/api-reference/introduction">
    Nordic stock exchanges (XSTO, XCSE, XHEL, XOSL, XICE) with listed companies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search" icon="magnifying-glass" color="#01B2FF" href="/api-reference/introduction">
    Full-text search across articles, stories, companies, countries, and exchanges.
  </Card>
</Columns>
