---
title: "Nordic Financial News API"
description: "Access translated Nordic business news, company data, and market stories through a REST API and MCP server."
---

Access translated Nordic business news, company data, and market stories through a simple REST API. All Nordic-language articles are automatically translated to English with AI-generated summaries, key points, and entity extraction.
